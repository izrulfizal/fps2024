Welcome to FPS2024!

2nd FSKTM Postgraduate Symposium

Vission

Empowering FSKTM postgraduate students to become trailblazers in the field of Information Technology, driving innovation and shaping the digital landscape of the future.

Mission

Nurturing a collaborative environment that fosters intellectual growth and equipping students with the skills and knowledge to excel in their respective areas of expertise.

Presenters

Every active postgraduate research student (KIT / PIT) who is not in deferment and is not doing proposal defense / pre-viva / viva in this semester, is required to participate in this Symposium.


Presenters are scheduled to 3 parallel sessions (Musk / Zuckerberg / Gates) each day. For more information regarding the schedule, click here.


All presenters are required to attend both opening and closing ceremony. Awards will be given out during the closing ceremony.
